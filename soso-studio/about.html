About Us

Who is SoSo Studio?

SoSo studio is a small, independent business operating out of Perth, WA. They create, and even design, high-quality custom rugs based on received commissions. The studio began when Daniel and his girlfriend Britt attended a rug-making workshop in 2021. Since then, they have actively continued it as a hobby and began to turn it into a business as of 2023.
